# Next Dose
Forget the math, just take your meds. Calculate your next dose time instantly.

## Philosophy
* **1 Tool, 1 Action.**
* **No Login / No Ads / No Tracking**
* **Browser-only Privacy** (Works on low-spec devices)

## How to Use
1. **Tap "I TOOK IT NOW"** when you take your medicine.
2. **Select the Interval** (e.g., 4h, 6h).
3. **Get the exact time** for your next dose displayed in large, clear text.
